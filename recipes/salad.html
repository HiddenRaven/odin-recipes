Salad

Description

This hefty salad packed with nutrients is a refreshing lunch for any day. A beautiful mix of sweet an savory with spicy chili pepper and arugula balanced with honey glazed sweet potato. The ultimate energy meal to keep healthy and to restore all vitamins and nutrients lost through daily exercise.

Ingredients

  • Arugula
  • Bean sprouts
  • Sweet potato
  • Honey
  • Chili pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Chop sweet potato and cover in honey, then place on baking sheet
  3. Bake sweet potato until golden brown
  4. Place arugula, bean sprouts, chili pepper, and cooked sweet potato into bowl and serve.
