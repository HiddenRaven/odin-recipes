Soup

Description

Comforting and healthy soup full of vegetables. This soup keeps you nourished and warm throughout all of winter. Since this soup contains a variety of vegetables everyone will get all of the vitamins and minerals needed in one meal. Easy and simple recipe for a quick and fulfilling dinner.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable broth
  • Onion
  • Cauliflower
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • Seaweed

Steps

  1. Chop all the vegetables
  2. Saute the vegetables in a pot
  3. Add vegetable broth and let simmer for 10 min
  4. Serve in bowl with bread if desired
